[INSTALLATION]

1. Upload the 'version_info' folder in this archive to your Symphony 'extensions' folder.

2. Enable it by selecting the "Version Info" entry, choose Enable from the with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3. You will now see your Symphony version number in the footer
